Fluffy French Toast

By Allrecipies member from Allrecipies

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Total: 30 mins
Servings: 12
Yield: 12 slices

Ingredients

  • Â¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 thick slices bread

Directions

  1. Step 1:
    Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in milk. Whisk in eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until smooth.
  2. Step 2:
    Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Step 3:
    Soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated.
  4. Step 4:
    Working in batches, cook bread on preheated griddle or pan until golden brown on each side. Serve hot.
